What We Do

We solve complex technology problems across multiple domains. From inventing and developing the world's first capacitive-touch smart phone in 2004, to creating advanced web and mobile applications, deeda's solutions consistantly predict the tech curve. Our IP portfolio also covers projects in aerospace and electric vehicle development. deeda develops and accelerates its projects with the support of Wilson Sonsini Goodrich and Rosati, and Silicon Valley Bank.
